I have personally used both the ASICS Jolt 4 running shoes and Nike downshifters series. I did 300 & 400 km with JOLT 4 & Downshifters respectively
These are my findings

  • Asics Jolt 4 & Nike downshifters both have good sole and comfort, but there's a bit more with JOLT 4 when it comes to sole
  • Jolt 4 provides a little bit better grip and sole compared to downshifters.
  • Asics looses with Downshifters in terms of breathability. If you run for say 3-5 km in the evenings, using Jolt 4, heat accumulates in the shoe
  • Downshifters has a outer mesh material from which heat escapes.
  • Downshifters are better for long running
  • ASICS jolt 4 are comparitively cheaper than Nike Downshifters. If you are able to grab any downshifter series for alost similar price as of JOLT 4, i personally feel you should go with Downshifters
